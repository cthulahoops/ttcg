# Characters:

## Implemented

1. **Frodo** - Win at least two ring cards (four in 3-player)
2. **Gandalf** - Win at least one trick
3. **Merry** - Win exactly one or two tricks
4. **Celeborn** - Win at least three cards of the same rank
5. **Pippin** - Win the fewest (or joint fewest) tricks
6. **Boromir** - Win the last trick; do NOT win the 1 of Rings
7. **Sam** - Win the Hills card matching your threat card
8. **Gimli** - Win the Mountains card matching your threat card
9. **Legolas** - Win the Forests card matching your threat card
10. **Aragorn** - Win exactly the number of tricks shown on your threat card
11. **Goldberry** - Win exactly three tricks in a row and no other tricks
12. **Glorfindel** - Win every Shadows card
13. **Galadriel** - Win neither the fewest nor the most tricks
14. **Gildor Inglorian** - Play a forests card in final trick
15. **Farmer Maggot** - Win at least two cards matching the threat card rank
16. **Fatty Bolger** - Win exactly one trick
17. **Tom Bombadil** - Win 3 or more cards matching the suit of a card left in hand at the end of round
18. **Bill the Pony** - Win exactly 1 trick. Setup: exchange simultaneously with Sam and Frodo.
19. **Elrond** - Every character must win a ring card. Setup: everyone simultaneously passes 1 card to the right.
20. **Arwen** - Win the most forests cards. Setup: Exchange with Elrond or Aragorn.
21. **Gloin** - Win the most mountains cards. Setup: Exchange with Bilbo or Gimli.
22. **Bilbo Baggins** - Win 3 or more tricks. Whenever Bilbo wins a trick, he may choose who leads the next trick. Must not win the 1 of rings. Setup: None
23. **Gwaihir** - Win at least two tricks containing a mountain card. Setup: Exchange with Gandalf twice.
24. **Shadowfax** - Win at least two tricks containing a hills card. Setup: Set one card from hand aside. It does not count as part of your hand, but you may choose to return it to hand at any point. Must be returned if your hand is empty.

## Unimplemented

25. Radogast - Win at least two tricks containing a forest card. Setup: set 1 card aside to be played into the final trick. Play 1 card into the first trick now.
